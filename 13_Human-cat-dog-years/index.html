Human, cat and dog years

Mubashir has a cat and a dog. He purchased them at the same time humanYears ago.


Create a function that takes an argument of humanYears and returns [humanYears, catYears, dogYears] array.

Human Years

  • HumanYears += 1
  • Human Years are whole numbers only.

Cat Years

  • 15 for the first cat year
  • +9 for the second year
  • +4 for each year after

Dog Years

  • 15 for the first dog year
  • +9 for the second year
  • +5 for each year after

Examples

        calculateYears(1) => [1, 15, 15]
        calculateYears(2) => [2, 24, 24]
        calculateYears(10) => [10, 56, 64]
